Fall in love with this updated and charming bungalow in a great location! There is plenty of room in this 1200+ square-foot house, with three bedrooms and one and a half baths, plus a potential fourth bedroom or office in the basement. Features include hardwood floors throughout, French glass doors leading to a newly fenced back yard, wood-burning fireplace, solid-surface countertops, plenty of kitchen cabinet space, original 1930s glass doorknobs, plus much more!

Save gas by taking advantage of its convenient location. Walking distance to St. John's Hospital, Delaware Elementary, parks and more, plus a short drive (or bike ride) to MSU and Downtown. Pets considered.
